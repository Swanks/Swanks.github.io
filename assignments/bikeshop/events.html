The Continintal Divide Trek

Come join the throng, for the annual tour of The Continental Divide Trek, on our annual tour ride, which is by far the most popular section of the longest off road route on the continent. The complete route is currently around 2,700 miles long and is touted to be the birthplace of the bikepacking sport. The route follows the continental divide from Mexico up into Canada, and is about 90% off-road.

The route is best known for the annual tour The Continental Divide Trek. The Montana section of the route is one of the most mountainous sections, with a great diversity of terrain. This section of the race is a 10 day event, and starts above Glacier National Park, and ends down by Lakeview.

The entrance fee is $600, and starts over the Labor Day weekend each year. The cost covers the entry fees, food, lodging, and support, and travel for the trip, and of course your team jerseys’.
