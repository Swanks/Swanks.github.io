Bicycle Sales

We are locally owned and operated. We feel that the most important part of purchasing a bike is the relationship that we establish with you on a long term basis. We are committed to offering you friendly professional experience regardless of your cycling goals. Your riding experience is important to us, so a complete customer fit and bike sizing is performed by a qualified technician as part of each purchase. Every bike we sell includes one year of free adjustments and labor on flat repairs for as long as you own the bike!
